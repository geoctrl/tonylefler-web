# Badge

A compact label component for displaying status, counts, or categories.

## Import

```tsx
import { Badge } from "root";
```

## Basic Usage

<Example>
  <div className="flex gap-2">
    <Badge>Default</Badge>
    <Badge intent="primary">New</Badge>
    <Badge intent="success">Active</Badge>
  </div>
</Example>

```tsx
<Badge>Default</Badge>
<Badge intent="primary">New</Badge>
<Badge intent="success">Active</Badge>
```

## Props

`<Badge>` accepts all [HTML div attributes](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/HTML/Element/div#attributes) and the following:

## Intent

Badge intents provide semantic meaning through color.

### `intent="grey"` (default)

Neutral badges for general labels or categories.

- **When to use:** Non-status labels, categories, tags, or neutral information.
- **Example:** User roles, content categories, filter tags.

<Example>
  <div className="flex gap-2">
    <Badge>Admin</Badge>
    <Badge>Member</Badge>
    <Badge>Guest</Badge>
  </div>
</Example>

```tsx
<Badge>Admin</Badge>
<Badge>Member</Badge>
<Badge>Guest</Badge>
```

### `intent="primary"`

Primary color accent for emphasis or branding.

- **When to use:** Highlighting new features, special items, or brand-related tags.
- **Example:** New releases, featured content, beta features.

<Example>
  <div className="flex gap-2">
    <Badge intent="primary">New</Badge>
    <Badge intent="primary">Featured</Badge>
    <Badge intent="primary">Beta</Badge>
  </div>
</Example>

```tsx
<Badge intent="primary">New</Badge>
<Badge intent="primary">Featured</Badge>
<Badge intent="primary">Beta</Badge>
```

### `intent="success"`

Positive status or successful states.

- **When to use:** Active status, completed tasks, success states, or positive indicators.
- **Example:** Active subscriptions, published content, verified status.

<Example>
  <div className="flex gap-2">
    <Badge intent="success">Active</Badge>
    <Badge intent="success">Published</Badge>
    <Badge intent="success">Verified</Badge>
  </div>
</Example>

```tsx
<Badge intent="success">Active</Badge>
<Badge intent="success">Published</Badge>
<Badge intent="success">Verified</Badge>
```

### `intent="warning"`

Cautionary states that need attention but aren't critical.

- **When to use:** Pending actions, items needing review, or temporary states.
- **Example:** Pending approval, expiring soon, trial periods.

<Example>
  <div className="flex gap-2">
    <Badge intent="warning">Pending</Badge>
    <Badge intent="warning">Expiring Soon</Badge>
    <Badge intent="warning">Trial</Badge>
  </div>
</Example>

```tsx
<Badge intent="warning">Pending</Badge>
<Badge intent="warning">Expiring Soon</Badge>
<Badge intent="warning">Trial</Badge>
```

### `intent="danger"`

Error states or critical issues requiring action.

- **When to use:** Errors, blocked states, critical warnings, or destructive actions.
- **Example:** Failed processes, blocked accounts, overdue items.

<Example>
  <div className="flex gap-2">
    <Badge intent="danger">Error</Badge>
    <Badge intent="danger">Blocked</Badge>
    <Badge intent="danger">Overdue</Badge>
  </div>
</Example>

```tsx
<Badge intent="danger">Error</Badge>
<Badge intent="danger">Blocked</Badge>
<Badge intent="danger">Overdue</Badge>
```

### `intent="info"`

Informational states or neutral notifications.

- **When to use:** Informational labels, tips, or secondary status indicators.
- **Example:** Draft content, archived items, informational tags.

<Example>
  <div className="flex gap-2">
    <Badge intent="info">Draft</Badge>
    <Badge intent="info">Archived</Badge>
    <Badge intent="info">Info</Badge>
  </div>
</Example>

```tsx
<Badge intent="info">Draft</Badge>
<Badge intent="info">Archived</Badge>
<Badge intent="info">Info</Badge>
```

<Example>
  <div className="flex items-center gap-2">
    <Badge circle size="sm">3</Badge>
    <Badge circle size="md">12</Badge>
    <Badge circle size="lg">99</Badge>
  </div>
</Example>

```tsx
<Badge circle size="sm">3</Badge>
<Badge circle size="md">12</Badge>
<Badge circle size="lg">99</Badge>
```
